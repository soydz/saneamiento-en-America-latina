Sanitation in Latin America

Project that takes statistics from the majority of Latin American countries from a file and makes some interesting statistics such as:

  • Average population affected by droughts
  • Average number of people with access to water
  • The country with the greatest access to water
  • Average population
  • Variance of people affected by droughts

    • Variance is a measure of dispersion used to measure how far data is from its average value.
